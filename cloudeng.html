Cloud engineer:


A cloud engineer is an IT professional responsible for any technological duties associated with cloud computing, including design, planning, management, maintenance and support.
Companies that hire cloud engineers are often looking to deploy cloud services or further their cloud understanding and technology.
Cloud engineers need to be familiar with programming languages including Java, Python and Ruby. A typical day includes making the environment safer, finding ways to automate processes, finding optimizations for cost andperformance, and responding to customer inquiries.
