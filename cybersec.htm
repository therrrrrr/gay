Cyber Security:


Cyber security is the realm of securing and protecting computer systems from a variety of threats, such as theft of
data or hardware, disruption of services or unauthorised access to data etc.

Cyber security is involved with all kinds of electronic devices that we use in our day to day lives, such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, and computers etc.

Cyberattacks are an evolving danger to organizations, employees and consumers.

They may be designed to access or destroy sensitive data or extort money.

They can, in effect, destroy businesses and damage your financial and personal lives; so
having sufficient cyber security is a must for anyone using an internet connected device.
A strong cyber security system has multiple layers of protection spread across computers, devices, networks and programs; so it will require someone to create and manage these protection systems.
This is where a cyber-security job comes into demand, and with the number of devices increasing dramatically all over the world,
the need for cyber security specialists will be much greater than they already are.
