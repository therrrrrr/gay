Artificial Intelligence Engineer:

Artificial intelligence engineers are people involved in problem-solving or Artificial Intelligence technology.
They create, test, and implement AI models, along with the handling of the AI infrastructure.

They cruise through traditional software models and Machine Learning models. Engineers may also choose between projects involving weak or strong artificial intelligence, where different setups focus on different capabilities.
AI professionals need to cultivate efficient problem solving, logic, analytical and communication skills to be successful.
